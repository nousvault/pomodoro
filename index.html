Pomodoro by NousVault

Pomodoro timer for focused work

Pomodoro by NousVault is a lightweight focus timer built for deep work, study sessions, and personal task management. It helps you run Pomodoro cycles in browser with focus rounds, short breaks, long breaks, task tracking, and simple session stats without needing an account.

This focus timer is designed to stay fast, visual, and low friction. Use it to break large tasks into smaller sessions, review your completed focus time, and keep momentum during work blocks from desktop or mobile.

Useful scenarios include study sprints, coding sessions, writing blocks, revision cycles, and quiet solo planning. The built-in task list and activity summary make this Pomodoro timer practical for people who want a simple workflow, visible progress, and no setup overhead.

Related projects: developer tools, free whiteboard, and NousVault homepage.
